ABOUT

Yoga 4 is a fresh online health conscious yoga video channel delivering quality instructional, educational and inspirational programs. Our vision is that you are empowered to choose self-healing tools through yoga, mindfulness and meditation. Our mission is to be Australia's leading online video channel delivering inspiring, informative and entertaining content that motivates you to make positive choices towards your well being.
